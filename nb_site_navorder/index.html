About Me

NB’s MarketSpace was born from family traditions, early‑morning market runs, and a love for real, fresh food. Now, I’m bringing that same freshness and care to busy families across Couva and Roystonia.

At NB’s MarketSpace, healthy living isn’t a trend — it’s how I grew up. Most of what we ate came straight from the land. My father planted everything he could: caraillee, barbadine, corn, callaloo bush and bodi. Every evening we would “pick” our tea — mint, fever grass, lime buds — and my mother always had fresh seasonings growing. Our kitchen was filled with the smell of real food.

Those simple moments shaped me. I learned to appreciate whole foods and the comfort of knowing where your food comes from. So when my brother started selling produce, something in me lit up. One morning at 3 a.m., he picked me up in Couva and took me to the market. He showed me the ropes, and that’s how NB’s MarketSpace began — with excitement, purpose, and a lot of heart.

This business is plenty of work, but it brings me joy. I love helping people. I love serving my community. And I believe in supporting busy families — whether you can’t make it to the market, or you just need an extra hand. If I can help you gain more time for your kids, for rest, or just for yourself, I’m happy to be there.

And I don’t do it alone. My children pitch in — weighing, packing, helping design flyers and the website. My husband is my rock, encourager, and steady support. I’m grateful for the love and teamwork behind every bag I deliver.

NB’s MarketSpace is more than produce. It’s family, community, and care you can feel. It’s the market delivered to your door — fresh, reliable, and personal.

Welcome to NB’s MarketSpace — where freshness meets heart.

What Makes NB’s MarketSpace Different

NB’s MarketSpace offers a personalised produce delivery service centred around freshness, reliability and care. All produce is hand‑selected, professionally stored and neatly packed for each customer.

Unlike large services, NB’s MarketSpace provides customised ordering, direct communication and consistent, trustworthy delivery within the Couva area. Customers enjoy clean, high‑quality produce with a friendly, dependable experience every week.
