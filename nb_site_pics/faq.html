Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions about ordering from NB’s MarketSpace. If you need anything else, feel free to reach out.

How do I place an order?

Orders are placed via WhatsApp or phone. Simply message or call Natasha at 759-1743 with your selected bag(s) or custom list.

When do orders close?

Orders close on Thursday for Saturday delivery (every other weekend). Cut-off dates are listed on the Bags & Prices page.

Where do you deliver?

Delivery is currently available in Couva and surrounding areas. If you’re unsure whether we deliver to you, just ask.

What is the delivery fee?

The standard delivery fee is TT$20.

Can I customise my order?

Yes. Custom orders are welcome, depending on availability. Just let us know what you need when placing your order.

Are prices fixed?

Prices may be updated weekly due to changes in market produce prices. The current price period is always shown on the website.

How is the produce selected?

All produce is hand-selected with care, stored properly, and packed neatly to ensure freshness and quality.

What if I have a question not listed here?

Feel free to call or WhatsApp Natasha at 759-1743. I’m always happy to help.
