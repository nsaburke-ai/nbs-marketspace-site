Frequently Asked Questions

Bag Options Why We’re Different
How do I place an order? ⌄
Orders are placed directly via WhatsApp. You can message Natasha at 759-1743.
What is the order cut-off time? ⌄
Orders close at Thursday at 7:00 p.m. In some cases, late orders may be accommodated on Friday. Where this applies, confirmation is provided directly.
What’s the difference between curated bags and custom orders? ⌄
Curated bags are pre-designed selections offered each delivery cycle, including options such as No-Fridge Essentials, Premium Bag, Seasonings & Salad, and Fruit Bag. Each bag is designed for practical household use, with items selected based on quality and market availability.

Custom orders are sourced individually based on your list. Where items fall outside the curated bags, retail pricing may apply. The total is confirmed before delivery.
How is pricing handled? ⌄
Curated bags are priced as complete packages.
Custom orders are priced as a whole, based on the items requested and retail market pricing.

In all cases, the total cost is shared for confirmation before delivery.
Why don’t you list individual item prices? ⌄
Produce quality and pricing can vary at the market from week to week. To maintain the standard our customers expect, orders are priced as a whole rather than by individual items.

This allows flexibility to select the best available produce and, where necessary, substitute or vary quantities. Customers are kept informed before delivery.
Can I request specific items or quantities? ⌄
Yes. Custom orders are welcome. Share your list, and the total will be confirmed once the selection is finalised.
When will I receive the final price? ⌄
The total is provided after your order is reviewed. Where market availability affects the final selection, the confirmed total is shared before delivery.
Do you offer subscriptions? ⌄
No. Orders are placed as needed. There are no subscriptions or standing commitments.
How is the produce selected? ⌄
Produce is selected individually based on quality and freshness, with care taken during handling and packing to ensure items arrive in good condition.
When are deliveries arranged? ⌄
Delivery typically takes place over the weekend. Exact timing is confirmed after orders are prepared and packed.
Can I request changes or substitutions? ⌄
Yes. Changes can be requested before the cut-off time, prior to market sourcing.

If you adjust a curated bag — by substituting items, increasing quantities, or removing items — it becomes a customised order.

Once the cut-off time has passed and sourcing begins, changes cannot be accommodated. If an item is unavailable or does not meet our quality standards, we will advise you before delivery.
What areas do you deliver to? ⌄
Delivery is focused in Couva and surrounding areas. Availability outside this area is confirmed on a case-by-case basis.
