How it works

A straightforward process with clean handoffs and clear expectations.

1) You place your order

Choose a bag, request specific items, or share a list. Where options exist, preferences are confirmed.

2) Produce is handpicked

Market selection is done with quality in mind—freshness, condition, and suitability for your household.

3) Cleaning, checks, and packing

Items are cleaned and packed carefully. Substitutions are confirmed before fulfilment.

4) Delivery window shared

Once packing is finalised, a delivery window is provided for smooth receipt.

5) Ready for your kitchen

Produce arrives organised and secure—so you can store, prep, or cook with less friction.
